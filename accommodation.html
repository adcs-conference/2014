Accommodation

Hotels

Low budget: The Tune hotel on Swanston St offers RMIT rates, with a double for $100 and a twin share for $110 per night. Mention RMIT in the booking and you'll receive the $11 per night utilities package for free (includes access to a television).

Medium budget: The Ibis hotel, between RMIT and the Queen Victoria Market, has an RMIT corporate rate of $130.

High budget: Rydges hotel, near University of Melbourne, has a corporate rate of $185 per night.

B&B, Apartments

If you prefer B&B or apartment accommodation, you could try AirBnB or Wimdu.
