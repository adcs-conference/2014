Location

ADCS 2014 will be held at the Swanston Academic Buidling (SAB) at RMIT University, Melbourne.

  • Paper sessions will be held in room 80.02.07 of the SAB (item (A) on the map).
  • Morning/afternoon tea and lunch will be served on the level 7 balcony of the SAB.
  • The poster session will be held at the Design Hub, level 4 (item (B) on the map).

Conference Dinner

The conference dinner will be held at the Clyde Hotel, from 7pm on Thursday (item (C) on the map).
